Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

अंतिम अपडेट: 03/07/2026

आयुष्मान भारत प्रधानमंत्री जन आरोग्य योजना

Benefit₹5 lakh / family / year
Who is eligibleEconomically weaker families & all 70+ seniors
Ministry / Dept.Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
Last dateOpen / ongoing

What is the scheme?

Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY is the world's largest health insurance scheme, giving eligible families up to ₹5 lakh of free hospital treatment per year.

Key Benefits

  • Up to ₹5 lakh free treatment per family per year
  • Cashless at govt & empanelled private hospitals
  • Pre- and post-hospitalisation costs covered
  • All 70+ seniors eligible

Eligibility (Who can apply?)

  • Families in the SECC 2011 list
  • All senior citizens aged 70+
  • No cap on family size or age

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card
  • Ration card
  • Mobile number

How to Apply

  • Check eligibility on pmjay.gov.in
  • Create card via Ayushman App e-KYC
  • Get card made at CSC or empanelled hospital
  • Show card at the time of treatment
