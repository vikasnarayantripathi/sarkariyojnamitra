Annapurna Bhandar Yojana

अंतिम अपडेट: 03/07/2026

अन्नपूर्णा भंडार योजना

Benefit₹3,000 / month
Who is eligibleWomen of West Bengal aged 25–60
Ministry / Dept.Women & Child Development & Social Welfare, West Bengal
Last dateCheck on portal (application window)

What is the scheme?

West Bengal's new flagship scheme (June 2026) giving eligible women ₹3,000 every month. It replaces the old 'Lakshmir Bhandar' scheme; existing beneficiaries are auto-migrated. New scheme — confirm on the portal.

Key Benefits

  • ₹3,000 every month (uniform for all categories)
  • Directly to Aadhaar-linked account (DBT)
  • Raised from Lakshmir Bhandar (₹1,500–1,700)
  • Existing beneficiaries auto-migrated

Eligibility (Who can apply?)

  • Permanent woman resident of West Bengal
  • Age 25–60
  • Income-tax payers/govt employees ineligible
  • Aadhaar-linked bank account

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar
  • West Bengal residence
  • Bank account (Aadhaar-linked)
  • Photo

How to Apply

  • Apply on socialsecurity.wb.gov.in (new beneficiaries)
  • Existing Lakshmir Bhandar beneficiaries auto-migrate
  • Amount on verification
