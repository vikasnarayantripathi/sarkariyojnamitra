PM Fasal Bima Yojana

प्रधानमंत्री फसल बीमा योजना

BenefitInsurance payout on crop loss
Who is eligibleFarmers growing notified crops
Ministry / Dept.Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
Last dateBefore the crop-season cut-off

What is the scheme?

PM Fasal Bima Yojana gives farmers insurance cover against crop loss from natural calamities, at a very low premium.

Key Benefits

  • Payout for loss from disaster, pests & disease
  • Farmer premium — Kharif 2%, Rabi 1.5%, commercial 5%
  • Govt bears the rest of the premium

Eligibility (Who can apply?)

  • Notified crop in a notified area
  • Both loanee & non-loanee farmers
  • KCC farmers auto-enrolled

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar
  • Land/sowing proof
  • Bank account
  • Crop details

How to Apply

  • Enrol via pmfby.gov.in or bank/CSC
  • Give crop & area details
  • Pay the premium
  • Report loss within 72 hours
